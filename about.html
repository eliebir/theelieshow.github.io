Welcome to The Elie Show.


The Elie Show Podcast brings you bi-weekly episodes of entertaining, informative, and honest discussions with creative industry leaders from around the world. Created and hosted by designer/director Ash Thorp, he aims to explore the struggles of work/life balance and to share the experiences of influential creatives from all realms of media, including (but not limited to) designers, illustrators, visual effects artists, writers, painters, and programmers.


This podcast is a collaborative effort between Markeef Trent, David Ross, our incredibly talented guests, and all of our wonderful fans. If you'd like to participate in The Elie Show community or promotional contests, be sure to visit our official Facebook page.


Earlier episodes of The Elie Show Podcast were produced and occasionally co-hosted by The Chui.


Feel free to reach out to us with any questions, comments, guest suggestions, media recommendations, or just to speak your thoughts. We'd love to hear from you on our contact page!
